Emotional Intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is your capacity for recognising and understanding your own emotions, and the emotions of others. It also encompasses the skills to use that recognition and understanding to connect with yourself and others more effectively.


How does EQ differ to IQ?

IQ refers to your capacity for learning and understanding new concepts. EQ refers specifically to the recognition and understanding of emotions.


Why is EQ important?

EQ is important for two main reasons. The first is that it helps you understand and work with yourself better. It's easy to be ruled by your emotions and not understand that it's happening. If you can recognise when you are experiencing negative emotions, you can work on ways to mitigate the negative impacts of that. If you're experiencing positive emotions, you can work on ways to flourish in that zone.

The second reason is that we all need to work cooperatively with others. If you can't recognise and understand the emotions of others, getting along with them and working collaboratively with them will be virtually impossible.
