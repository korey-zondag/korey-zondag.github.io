My Learning Plan

Long Term Goal

My long term goal is to become a developer, whether that is working full-time for a company or freelance as a contractor. I would like to begin by becoming a full-stack web developer. The skills I gained as a business analyst and junior product owner will combine well with skills gained as a developer to ensure a career in IT.

Learning Strengths and Limitations

My learning strengths include reading comprehension, curiosity and openness to new ideas, and a thirst for applicable knowledge.
Some of my limitations with regards to learning include being easily distracted, going down non-relevant rabbit holes of information, and not always communicating my need for help when I really need it.

Human Skills to Develop

There are three main human skills I would like to develop further while at Dev Academy. They are communication, time-management, and leadership. I sometimes have had the tendency to go off by myself and try to do a whole project alone, and not effectively communicate my timelines and when I needed help. I would like to develop my communication skills so that my team is aware of what I'm working on, and when they can expect work to be done.
I would also like to further develop leadership skills in planning and project delivery, which are skills I began to develop while working in product development.

Commitment to Managing Workload

During bootcamp I plan to split the 40 hours a week recommended time into normal business day hours (9am-5pm, Monday-Friday). If I need to work past 5pm, or during the weekend to get something done individually or as part of a team, I am more than willing to do that, either in-person or online.
I plan on attending campus regularly, with the exact times to be discussed with my homegroup. I am flexible and amenable to working both in-person and online at different times.
If something comes up and I need to take time away from a project, I will communicate that to anyone I am working with in a timely way, and work with them to manage both of our workloads fairly.

Commitment to Seeking Help

I commit to always being open to discussions, democratic with teamwork and team decisions, respectful, and professional. If I commit to a deadline, I will always endeavour to meet that deadline. I commit to effectively managing my emotions when things are building up and starting to get to me. If I need a break, I will take a break, and I will communicate that.
When I need help and cannot solve the problem on my own in a timely way, I will seek help from my home-group, either in-person or via Discord. If my home-group cannot help me, I will seek help from facilitators via Discord.

Expectations from Facilitators

I expect the Facilitation team to be professional, to answer queries and technical questions in a timely and thorough way, to provide insights into the professional IT world that would be foreign to new-comers, to be gracious and patient when dealing with students, and to be available during normal business hours.
