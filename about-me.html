Kia ora! I'm Korey, a Full Stack Web Developer who made the transition from Business Analysis. After working closely with software developers for over 5 years, I decided that what they were doing looked like great fun, and decided that building the future in code is what I wanted to do.

I am a graduate of Dev Academy Aotearoa, where I was trained to solve technical problems solo, in a pair, and in groups, using a variety of tools including TypeScript/JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, and more. I'm a big believer in maintaining a growth mindset, which has allowed me to learn a lot in a short amount of time.

My background in customer service and service management means I focus on the end-user's experience, and I love to work collaboratively. My experience as a Business Analyst means I plan methodically, make decisions based on evidence, and I believe the details matter.

I am currently building Chorely, a web application that will allow users to manage and track their chores with other users.
